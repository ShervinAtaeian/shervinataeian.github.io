Introduction

I am an analytical and detail-oriented Transportation Engineer and Data Scientist with a strong foundation in mobility analytics, holding an MSc in Industrial Engineering and soon to be graduated with a PhD in Transport Engineering. Proven experience in data analysis, mathematical modelling, and the application of Python for transport and mobility-related projects. Skilled in transport planning concepts, statistical analysis, and cross-functional collaboration. Experienced in defining and analyzing transport performance metrics and KPIs to inform data-driven decision-making. Extensive experience working with network-level transit data from different countries, contributing to applied research and real-world mobility insights. Bilingual in English, Farsi, Azerbaijani, and familiar with basic Spanish, with a deep interest in leveraging big data to improve urban mobility systems.
